BACKGROUND

The Why

Lunchatron was developed in under 25 hours at ConUHAck IX. The idea sparked from a simple question: What can I cook with what I already have? As students, we often find ourselves staring at random ingredients with no clue what to make.

With this in mind, we set out to build something fast, fun, and functional. The time constraint pushed us to brainstrom rapidly, prototype efficiently, and work collaboratively across frontend, backend, and AI components to bring Lunchatron to life. Lunchatron is an app that scans ingradients and instantly suggests recipes
